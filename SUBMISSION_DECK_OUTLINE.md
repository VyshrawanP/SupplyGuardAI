## Slide 2: The Problem (The "Logistics Blackout")
- **Pain Point**: Communication infrastructure fails exactly when it's needed most.
- **Statistics**: Mention that 90% of cellular towers can fail in flood/earthquake zones.
- **Impact**: Delayed rescue, supply chain collapse, and "supply-chain blindness."

## Slide 3: The Solution (The Resilience Stack)
- **Concept**: A triple-layered communication system that never goes dark.
- **Layers**:
    1. **Cloud**: Global Command.
    2. **Field**: Local Coordination.
    3. **Mesh**: P2P Offline Survival.
- **Value Prop**: 100% operational visibility even with zero internet.

## Slide 4: Key Feature - Offline Mesh Networking
- **Technology**: Bluetooth LE & Wi-Fi Direct Mesh.
- **User Story**: A victim sends an SOS from a buried basement; it hops through three phones to reach a rescue worker's tablet.
- **Visual**: A "node-to-node" hopping diagram.

## Slide 5: Key Feature - AI Risk & Route Optimization
- **Technology**: Real-time Risk Scoring Engine + Route Optimizer.
- **Value**: Automatically redirects supply trucks *before* they enter a danger zone.
- **AI Explainer**: Human-readable AI decisions via Google Gemini.

## Slide 6: Key Feature - Digital Twin Simulation
- **Technology**: State-cloning simulation engine.
- **Value**: Allows commanders to run "What-If" scenarios safely before executing in the real world.
- **Visual**: Side-by-side view of "Current State" vs "Simulated Impact."
