## 1. Problem Statement
**The Logistics Blackout in Disaster Zones**

In the immediate aftermath of natural disasters—floods, cyclones, or earthquakes—the critical "Golden 72 Hours" are often lost due to a total collapse of communication infrastructure. When cell towers, fiber optic cables, and power grids fail, traditional logistics systems go blind. 

Rescue teams lose real-time visibility into victim locations, and supply chains become paralyzed, unable to reroute around destroyed roads or coordinate drone dispatches. This "Logistics Blackout" leads to avoidable loss of life and inefficient resource allocation exactly when every minute counts.

---

## 2. Solution Overview
**SupplyGuard AI: The Resilient Disaster Logistics Ecosystem**

SupplyGuard AI is a comprehensive platform designed to maintain operational coordination when traditional networks fail. Our solution provides a **Triple-Layered Resilience Stack**:

1.  **Cloud Command Center**: A high-fidelity React-based dashboard for global situational awareness, integrated with AI for risk scoring and path optimization.
2.  **Field Operations App**: A Flutter-based mobile application that provides field agents with offline-first maps, local task management, and synchronization capabilities.
3.  **Resilient Mesh (Native Android)**: A decentralized P2P mesh network using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) and Wi-Fi Direct. This allows victims to send SOS signals and rescuers to coordinate without any cellular or internet service.

**Integrated Intelligence**: 
-   **Risk Engine**: Calculates real-time danger scores (0-100) based on environmental data.
-   **Route Optimizer**: Dynamically reroutes supply vehicles away from high-risk disaster zones.
-   **AI Explainer**: Uses Google Gemini to generate human-readable rationales for every automated decision, ensuring transparency for commanders.

SupplyGuard AI ensures that even if the world goes dark, the mission of saving lives continues uninterrupted.
