# 🎬 Demo Video Storyboard: SupplyGuard AI

**Total Duration**: 2-3 Minutes
**Goal**: Show, don't just tell. Demonstrate the "Mesh" and "AI" working in sync.

---

### Scene 1: The Crisis (0:00 - 0:30)
- **Visual**: Open the **Command Center Dashboard**. Zoom in on a map showing a disaster zone (e.g., a flood in Bangalore).
- **Action**: Use the **Simulation Engine** to trigger a "Bridge Collapse."
- **Narrative**: "A disaster hits. Infrastructure is failing. The bridge on NH-44 is down, and supply trucks are heading straight for it."

### Scene 2: The Blackout (0:30 - 1:00)
- **Visual**: Show the "Offline Mode" toggle on the dashboard. Map markers start turning red/offline.
- **Narrative**: "Communication is lost. Standard GPS and Cloud services are unreachable. In most systems, this is where the story ends."

### Scene 3: The Mesh Hero (1:00 - 1:45)
- **Visual**: Switch to a recording of the **Android Victim App**. Tap the "SOS" button.
- **Visual**: Switch to the **Rescue App**. Show the SOS alert popping up *instantly* via Bluetooth Mesh.
- **Narrative**: "But with SupplyGuard's Mesh Layer, the victim's SOS hops through nearby devices. No internet? No problem. The rescue team receives the signal and the exact location."

### Scene 4: AI Intervention (1:45 - 2:30)
- **Visual**: Back to the **Command Dashboard** (now synced back via the mesh gateway).
- **Action**: Show the **Route Optimizer** recalculating a truck's path.
- **Visual**: Click the "Explain Decision" button to show the **Gemini AI** output.
- **Narrative**: "Our AI Risk Engine detects the hazard and automatically reroutes the fleet. Gemini AI explains the rationale to the commander, ensuring human-in-the-loop safety."

### Scene 5: The Conclusion (2:30 - 3:00)
- **Visual**: Montage of the different layers (Web, Flutter, Android).
- **Narrative**: "SupplyGuard AI: Ensuring that even when the network fails, the mission doesn't. Resilient. Intelligent. Life-saving."
- **Visual**: Final Slide with GitHub Repo Link.

---

### 💡 Recording Tips:
1. **Screen Recording**: Use OBS or QuickTime. Ensure the resolution is high.
2. **Device Sync**: Use a tool like **Scrcpy** to mirror your Android phone to your PC so you can record both the Web Dashboard and the Mobile App in the same frame.
3. **Audio**: Use a clear microphone. If you're not comfortable with your voice, use a high-quality AI text-to-speech tool like ElevenLabs.
